## Travel Information for PyConf Hyderabad 2025 in Hyderabad

Welcome to **PyConf Hyderabad 2025** in Hyderabad! Below you'll find detailed travel information on how to reach the conference venue.

> Disclaimer: The information provided here is for general guidance and may be subject to change. This is intended to help attendees plan their travel to Hyderabad and navigate the city during the conference. For the most up-to-date information, please refer to official sources and local authorities.

### üó∫Ô∏è Getting to Hyderabad

#### ‚úàÔ∏è By Plane - Rajiv Gandhi International Airport

If you're arriving by air, the Rajiv Gandhi International Airport serves as your gateway to Hyderabad. Various Indian airlines operate flights to this airport, including Air India, Air India Express, Indian Airlines, Indigo Airlines, Trujet, and SpiceJet.

There is excellent connectivity from the airport to Hi-Tech City. You can either take a cab or use the government-run AC buses, "**Pushpak**," which are available 24 hours a day.

#### üöÜ By Train - Secunderabad or Hyderabad

For those opting for train travel, Indian Railways offer services to Hyderabad from different parts of India. Major railway stations include Secunderabad, Hyderabad, Kachiguda, and Begumpet. Hyderabad Deccan Station, also known as Nampally Station, is a significant hub for trains to South and North India via Secunderabad.

#### üöò By Car

Hyderabad is well-connected to other major cities by road. Routes like the Bangalore-Hyderabad corridor have been upgraded to four-lane divided highways, providing a convenient option for road travellers.

#### üöç By Bus

Hyderabad's extensive bus network, including state government and private buses, connects the city to various parts of Telangana, South India, and Western India. Major bus stations include JBS (Jubilee Bus Station) in Secunderabad and MGBS (Imliban), which holds the distinction of being one of the largest bus stations in the world.

For more comprehensive travel information and options, please refer to the [Hyderabad Wiki Page](https://wikitravel.org/en/Hyderabad#Get_in).

### Getting to the Conference & Workshop Venues

* The conference will be held at **T-Hub Phase 2**, Madhapur ([map](https://maps.app.goo.gl/7Ey8qqYmpvMzt5mx8)).
* The workshop will be held at **EPAM Systems, Knowledge City** ([map](https://maps.app.goo.gl/JJXrbhi4fyPfA99e8)).

Here are the different ways to reach the venue:

#### üöñ By Cab

You can book a cab from the airport, railway station, or any part of the city to reach T-Hub Phase 2 or EPAM Systems. Popular cab services like Uber, Ola, Rapido and Meru are available in Hyderabad.

#### üöÜ Via Hyderabad Metro

T-Hub Phase 2 and EPAM Systems are best accessible via the Hyderabad Metro üîµ Blue line. You can get down at the "Raidurg" stop.

Now, you can do a brief walk (10-12 mins) to T-Hub Phase 2 and EPAM Systems or use cab-sharing services for a ride.

- **From the üî¥ Red Line:** If you're arriving via the Red line, you can easily switch to the Blue line (towards Raidurg) at Ameerpet station.
- **From the üü¢ Green Line:** If you're coming via the Green line, you can switch to the Blue line at the Parade Grounds stop.
